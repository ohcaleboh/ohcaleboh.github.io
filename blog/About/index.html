Purpose

The stories of our lives matter. For the longest time, I thought only the stories of famous or historical people's lives mattered. But, that isn't true. All of our stories matter.

Our stories -- or at least the ones we tell ourselves -- shape our identities and values in life. They determine the person that we are becoming. The stories we tell ourselves shapes our perspective, attitude, and what we make of our lives.

In this website, I share some stories that have come to shape my life. I also share the stories of others. I hope that some of these stories can reveal parts of your own story and purpose in life.
